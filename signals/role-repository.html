Signals from RoleRepositoryÔÉÅ

The lback.repositories.role_repository.RoleRepository manages direct database interactions for Role entities. This repository emits signals at crucial stages of create, update, and delete operations, offering essential hooks for auditing role changes, triggering custom business logic, or synchronizing role assignments across different application components.
